About Darshan

Darshan Kalola is an undergraduate pre-med student at The College of New Jersey, where he studies Computer Science.

Since a young age, he has always wanted to pursue a career in medicine. However, in his Freshman year of college, he enrolled in an introductory computer science course. He enjoyed it so much he decided to switch his major, becoming the first Computer Science Seven Year Medical Student at TCNJ.

Darshan has completed internships at companies such as AT&T and Google, as well as done research at Weill Cornell Medical College. He will be attending New Jersey Medical School in the fall of 2020.

His interests are in Electronic Health Records (EHR), AI in medicine, and Internet of Things. He plays table tennis regularly and is an opinions contributor to The Signal, the TCNJ campus newspaper.
